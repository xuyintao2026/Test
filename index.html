3. "About Nest"--Can't find "Skills & Games"?

No worries—Alexa's interface has updated, and the path has changed a bit. Here's the now, exact path for the latest version:

  • Open the Alexa app → tap the menu icon (≡) in the top-left corner;
  • Select Explore Alexa+ Services;
  • Tap Skills & Games;
  • Search for Google Nest to enable the skill.
